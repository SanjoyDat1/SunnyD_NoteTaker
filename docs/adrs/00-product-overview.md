# Product overview — SunnyD Notes

## What it is

SunnyD Notes is a privacy-first, AI note-taking app. It reads your notes as you write and
surfaces contextual suggestions — fact checks, expansions, clarity improvements, research
citations, and lecture-based additions. There is no SunnyD backend: notes live in the browser,
and your API key is sent only to the AI provider you choose.

## Who it is for (target segments)

- **University students** — the primary persona. Notes can be tagged with subject, professor,
  and study goal, and Lecture mode transcribes lectures live and suggests additions. The
  Research feature surfaces real citations (DOI, PubMed, journals).
- **Researchers and analysts** — people who need fact-checked, well-cited notes.
- **Meeting-heavy professionals** — the Google Workspace integration turns notes into calendar
  events, meeting invites, and Gmail/Docs/Sheets drafts.

## Positioning

SunnyD is "an intelligent notebook that works for you while you write" — distinct from generic
AI chat because suggestions are inline, grounded in the note you are actually writing, and
verified against the source text. The privacy stance (no backend, bring-your-own-key) is a core
differentiator.
